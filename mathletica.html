What is a bookmarklet?

A bookmarklet is a small JavaScript program saved as a browser bookmark. When you click it on any webpage, it runs instantly — no extensions, no installs. These are built specifically for syllabus.nesa.nsw.edu.au and the official NESA HSC Mathematics pages, letting you pull curriculum outcomes directly into your study workflow.

1
Show your bookmark barPress Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) to make it visible.
2
Drag to the barClick and drag the gold button below directly into your bookmark bar.
3
Visit a NESA pageGo to any HSC Mathematics syllabus page on syllabus.nesa.nsw.edu.au.
4
Click the bookmarkletHit it in your bar — the script activates on the current page instantly.
